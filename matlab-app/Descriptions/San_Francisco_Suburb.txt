A simplified, custom load profile for a hypothetical suburban area on the outskirts of San Francisco. Comprises load profle data for 60 residential consumers, primary and secondary schools, hopsitals, OPDs, midrise apartments, restaurants, and supermarkets aggregated by hour over the same 24 hour period. These load profiles are from the US Department of Energy's Open Data Catalog, which provides a year of kWh consumption data for 16 standardised commercial building types as well as residential consumers for all TMY3 locations in the US.
